Interstellar

Intersellar is a Sci-Fi movie that is set in a dystopian future in which the Earth is about to end due to climate change, a global food crisis, and dust storms. We follow the main character Cooper(played by Matthew McConaughey) and a group of astronauts as they're sent on a mission to find a new planet in another galaxy for huumanity to migrate to in order to survive.
